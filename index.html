About Us

BUSS is the central body through which all bioengineering student activities are organized. Our executive council is composed of 14 dynamic and dedicated bioengineering undergraduate students that are each as unique as the next. We are all connected through a common desire to enhance the experience of all students within the bioengineering program. Dedicated to the bioengineering experience at McGill, we try to make our small department into what we believe it to be: a close knit family of hard-working students.
